The Balenciaga Track is a truly iconic sneaker model from the brand. It features a robust ("chunky") design, crafted with various material layers (mesh, nylon, synthetic leather, among others), and a thick, highly detailed sole. Visually, it blends influences from mountain running shoes, hiking boots, and a futuristic style. The retail price for the Balenciaga Track (new) currently ranges between $895 – $1,150 USD (depending on the exact model, colorways, and materials).

The Balenciaga Biker Boots are an iconic luxury item, known for their exaggerated and chunky design. Inspired by motocross boots, they feature thick, toothed rubber soles and a voluminous silhouette. They represent the brand's utilitarian and subversive aesthetic, serving as the focal point of any outfit. Currently costing $1,250 – $4,900 USD (depending on the model, material, and height).
